# Project Overview

## What is Hullah?

**Hullah** (Arabic for "elegance" or "attire") is an AI-powered mobile application designed to help women choose abayas that match their body shape, accurately and effortlessly.

The abaya is more than a garment — it is a prominent symbol of Gulf identity and is worn daily by many women across Saudi Arabia and the wider region. While the abaya has evolved to incorporate modern fashion trends, finding one that fits well remains a persistent challenge, particularly when shopping online or through social media.

Hullah was created to bridge the gap between **digital shopping and personalized garment fitting**, using artificial intelligence to recommend abaya styles based on a user's actual body proportions rather than generic, standardized sizing.

## Background

Saudi Arabia is recognized as a hub for abaya design, with a high concentration of designers producing a wide variety of styles. Despite this, most shopping experiences — especially online — rely on standardized sizing that does not reflect the diversity of real body shapes.

Hullah's core idea is simple: let users either **manually enter their body measurements** or **upload a single front-facing photo**, and let AI handle the rest — extracting measurements, classifying body shape, and recommending suitable abaya designs.

## Aim

The aim of Hullah is to support women who wear abayas by providing a personalized abaya recommendation experience. By leveraging AI and deep learning technologies, the project automates body measurement analysis and abaya selection, reducing human error in sizing and promoting inclusivity within the abaya industry.

## Objectives

The project was guided by the following core objectives:

1. Use a pre-trained AI model to extract body measurements from uploaded images.
2. Convert pixel-based measurements into accurate centimeter values.
3. Classify body shapes using anthropometric standards.
4. Recommend suitable abaya styles based on body shape and user preferences.

## Scope

Hullah focuses on providing an intelligent, personalized abaya recommendation system for women in Saudi Arabia. The application:

- Allows users to upload a clear image **or** manually input body measurements.
- Extracts or processes these measurements to determine body type.
- Recommends abaya designs from a pre-defined, curated catalog based on body shape.

**Out of scope:** direct purchasing functionality, real-time tailoring services, and the creation of new abaya designs. The system recommends from existing, curated styles rather than designing new garments.

## Target Users

Hullah primarily targets:

- **Women who wear abayas** and want to find designs that complement their body shape, whether shopping online or simply exploring style options.
- **Abaya designers and boutique owners**, who can use the insights to better understand customer body profiles and tailor their offerings accordingly.

Beyond its practical use, Hullah also has an educational dimension — helping users become more aware of their body shape and make confident, informed fashion choices.
